Welcome to the Doula Cooperative of Rochester, NY

We are a collective of experienced, compassionate doulas serving families throughout Rochester and the surrounding communities. Our cooperative model ensures you'll find the right support for your unique needs and life transitions.

We believe birth support should be accessible, personalized, and rooted in community. Each doula brings their own expertise while sharing our commitment to evidence-based care and honoring your choices.

We offer comprehensive support from prenatal education to labor support, from postpartum care to specialized services, helping you feel confident throughout your journey into parenthood.

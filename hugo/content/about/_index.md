**Metadata over Dogma** is an unofficial companion site for the [Data Over Dogma](https://www.youtube.com/@dataoverdogma) podcast, hosted by Dr. Dan McClellan and Dan Beecher.

## What we do

This site provides searchable, browseable access to the metadata surrounding each episode — transcripts, topic tags, scripture references, recurring segments, and guest appearances. Our goal is to make the scholarly content of the podcast easier to find, reference, and revisit.

## How it works

Episodes are transcribed using AI-assisted tools, then corrected and tagged through a combination of automated and manual processes. Every transcript is reviewed for accuracy, and topics are mapped to a controlled vocabulary to keep the archive consistent and searchable.

## Disclaimer

This is an independent fan project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Data Over Dogma, Dan McClellan, or Dan Beecher. All podcast content remains the property of its original creators.

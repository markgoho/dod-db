Preterism comes up on the show as one way readers make sense of biblical
apocalyptic texts by locating their fulfillment in the first century rather
than in our future. It becomes especially important when discussing Revelation,
the destruction of Jerusalem, the Olivet Discourse, and the difference between
confessional readings of prophecy and critical historical analysis.
